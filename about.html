About

Khogendra Rupini is the founder of Levoric Learn, an open-source educational platform. After completing his 12th standard, he decided not to pursue college and instead focused on building and developing Levoric Learn. As a skilled web designer and front-end expert, Khogendra specializes in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Additionally, he has a strong understanding of backend technologies, including PHP, MySQL, and database management.

At just 19 years old, Khogendra has taken on the role of lead developer and primary contributor to Levoric Learn, dedicating his time to expanding and improving the platform. His passion for education and development continues to drive the growth of Levoric Learn, making it an invaluable resource for learners and developers worldwide.
